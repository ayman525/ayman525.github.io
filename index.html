FRENCH OMELET

  • Time: 5m
  • Serves: 2m
Ingredients Quantity
egg 2
water 2 tbsp
salt 1/8 tsp
pepper Dash
butter 1 tsp
filling, finely chopped ham 1/3 cup

Steps:
1-BEAT eggs, water, salt and pepper in small bowl until blended.

2-HEAT butter in 6 to 8-inch nonstick omelet pan or skillet over medium-high heat until hot. TILT pan to coat bottom. POUR IN egg mixture. Mixture should set immediately at edges.

3-GENTLY PUSH cooked portions from edges toward the center with inverted turner so that uncooked eggs can reach the hot pan surface. CONTINUE cooking, tilting pan and gently moving cooked portions as needed.

4-When top surface of eggs is thickened and no visible liquid egg remains, PLACE filling on one side of the omelet. FOLD omelet in half with turner. With a quick flip of the wrist, turn pan and INVERT or SLIDE omelet onto plate. SERVE immediately.

Smooth Sweet Tea

  • Time: 5m
  • Serves: 3h
Ingredients Quantity
pinch baking soda 1
cups boiling water 2
tea bags 6
cup white sugar 3/4
cups cool water 6

Steps:
1-Sprinkle a pinch of baking soda into a 64-ounce, heat-proof, glass pitcher. Pour in boiling water, and add tea bags. Cover, and allow to steep for 15 minutes.

2-Remove tea bags, and discard; stir in sugar until dissolved. Pour in cool water, then refrigerate until cold.

Orange juice

  • Time: 5
  • Ready: 5
Ingredients Quantity
oranges 4

Step:
Lightly smack each orange on the counter. Cut each one in half. Squeeze into a glass. You may also use a citrus reamer to do this. If you want less pulp, use a hand juicer with a strainer.
